4 Binary Choice

Binary choice models (\(y\) takes two values: 0 or 1)

  • Did you vote during the last election?
  • Does an individual recidivate after being released from prison?
  • Participation in the labor market
  • Purchasing a home
  • Model: \(Pr(y=1|x)\)

Categorical dependent variable but naturally ordered

  • What is your level of happiness? E.g., very happy, happy, ok, sad.

Categorical dependent variable but no ordering

  • How do you commute to campus?
  • Did you vote for the democratic, republican, or independent candidate during the last election?
bhat_logit = glm(buying~income,family=binomial(link="logit"),data=organic)
